Design Principles Document

Target Audience

The target audience for the website are people who live in areas where they experience extreme changes in weather, either hot or cold., also people that lives in big cities. And anyone who are interested on the climate subject and the purpose of the website.

Personas

  • Persona 1: George - George is a 60-year-old violin teacher who regularly accesses the internet from his personal desktop over a broadband connection.

  • Persona 2: Georgina - Georgina is a 29-year-old ad exec who is connected to the internet all day. She confidently uses her Macbook, iPad, and Android phone to access the web - whatever is to hand.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: People who are looking for opportunities to learn about the weather.

  • Scenario 2: People who are looking for answers about why the weather is changing a lot.

  • Scenario 3: People who are looking for answers about the future of the earth and what they can do to help.

  • Scenario 4: People who are looking for groups of people to interact and learn together about the weather,

  • Scenario 5: People who are looking for groups to share knowledge,
